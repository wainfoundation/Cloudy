Code of Conduct

At Cloudy, we strive to create a respectful, inclusive, and safe community for all users. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for behavior on our platform.

Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering a welcoming environment where everyone can create, collaborate, and thrive. This applies to all interactions on Cloudy, including workspaces, product listings, community forums, and support channels.

Expected Behavior

  • Be respectful and considerate of others, regardless of differences in background, identity, or opinions.
  • Use inclusive language and avoid offensive, discriminatory, or harassing remarks.
  • Provide constructive feedback and engage in positive, collaborative discussions.
  • Respect intellectual property and only share content you have the right to use.
  • Follow our Terms of Service and applicable laws.

Unacceptable Behavior

  • Harassment, bullying, or intimidation in any form, including threats or personal attacks.
  • Posting or sharing inappropriate, offensive, or illegal content (e.g., hate speech, explicit material).
  • Spamming, trolling, or disrupting the community with irrelevant or malicious content.
  • Impersonating others or misrepresenting your identity.
  • Violating the privacy of others by sharing personal information without consent.

Enforcement

We take violations of this Code of Conduct seriously. If you engage in unacceptable behavior, we may:

  • Issue a warning and request corrective action.
  • Temporarily suspend your account or restrict access to certain features.
  • Permanently ban your account for repeated or severe violations.

Decisions are made at the discretion of the Cloudy team and are final.

Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to us:

  • Email: support@cloudy.com
  • Contact Form: Contact Us
  • Community Moderators: Reach out via our Community page.

We will investigate reports confidentially and take appropriate action.
